West Side Sim-Racing Club 2022 GT4 Championship Series 1.

01/02/22

West Side Sim-Racing is pleased to present our first Championship Series for 2022! We return to the exciting GT4 class, featuring reverse grids! Race 1 will have a P/Q/R format, after which all remaining races the grid start order will be based on the overall standings, reversed!

A total of 6 races on an exciting track selection, one hour races, with a single mandatory pit stop and a 20 minute window. These races will be intense and strategic!
