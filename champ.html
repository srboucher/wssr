West Side Sim-Racing Club 2022 GT3 Championship Series 2

03/10/22

West Side Sim-Racing is pleased to present our second championship series for 2022. A return to the familiar comfort of a GT3 cockpit, featuring reversed grids based on overall championship standings, and the introduction of variable weather conditions for two events. The first 3 races will feature a standard P/Q/R format, after which the remaining 7 races will be reverse grid.

A 10 race championship series featuring an exciting track selection, custom reversed grids, and the top class GT3 cars!

West Side Sim-Racing Club 2022 GT4 Championship Series 1.

01/02/22

West Side Sim-Racing is pleased to present our first Championship Series for 2022! We return to the exciting GT4 class, featuring reverse grids! Race 1 will have a P/Q/R format, after which all remaining races the grid start order will be based on the overall standings, reversed!

A total of 6 races on an exciting track selection, one hour races, with a single mandatory pit stop and a 20 minute window. These races will be intense and strategic!
